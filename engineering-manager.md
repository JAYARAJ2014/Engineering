# Engineering Manager Responsibilities

Engineering Managers operate across three core pillars:

1. **People Leadership**  
2. **Delivery & Execution**  
3. **Technical Direction & Quality**  


---

## 1. People Leadership

### 1.1 Build, grow, and mentor the team
- Conduct regular 1:1s  
- Coach engineers on soft and hard skills  
- Create growth and development plans  
- Identify skill gaps and training needs  

### 1.2 Hiring and team scaling
- Write job descriptions  
- Conduct interviews  
- Build a diverse and balanced team  
- Onboard new engineers effectively  

### 1.3 Performance management
- Set clear expectations  
- Identify and address performance issues early  
- Provide constructive feedback  
- Manage promotions and PIPs when required  

### 1.4 Foster psychological safety & team health
- Encourage open communication  
- Remove toxic behavior  
- Promote trust and collaboration  
- Facilitate conflict resolution
